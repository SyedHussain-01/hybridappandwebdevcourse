 ABOUT RESTAURANT 

QUALITY

Welcome to the Restaurant. We have been providing fresh, fast, and friendly food to Karachi since 2020. Restaurant isn’t just about authentic Pakistani food, it specializes in high standard, fresh and healthy food with affordable prices. Our food is cooked fresh to the customer’s order and taste. You are guaranteed the freshness of your order.

REPUTATION

Restaurant is one of Pakistan's most beloved restaurants, It has welcomed guests to enjoy its contemporary Pakistani cuisine, warm hospitality, and unparalleled service in Karachi for a long time. Our Chef's ever-evolving seasonal menu showcases the restaurant's relationships with the citizens of the city.

ATMOSPHERE

Enjoyable and comfortable atmosphere in the heart of Karachi. Restaurant offers 50 seating places, surrounding a big fireplace that will make your dining exquisite and enjoyable. Smart interior design will make you feel like home. Royal plate can host large groups too, so if you need more seats for your feast simply send us an email, or call us by phone.

NATURAL SURROUNDINGS

Enjoy your dining at Restaurant terrace, surrounded by natural wooden fence and plenty of green. Royal plate terrace offers 72 seating places which can be rearranged for large groups and special events like weddings, corporate parties or birthday parties .
